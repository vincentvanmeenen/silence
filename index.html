Vincent Van Meenen

Award-Winning Crypto-Native Author
BNG Bank Literature Prize Shortlist • Published by Das Mag

From Crypto Degen to Literary Voice

Vincent Van Meenen dove into the crypto world three years ago, lived the degen experience firsthand, and emerged with an award-winning novel that captured the psychological reality of digital gold rush culture.

Now a mainstream literary success, he bridges crypto culture and European literature, translating the complexity of blockchain communities for broader audiences while maintaining deep respect for the technology's transformative potential.

0xBlixa

🏆 BNG Bank Literature Prize Shortlist
DeFi Ethereum Wallet Hack Crypto Twitter Angel Investing FOMO Culture Digital Identity

The first literary novel to authentically capture crypto culture from the inside. Follow Paul (0xBlixa), a 31-year-old venture capitalist who made a fortune young but struggles with the psychological toll of the cryptoverse.

Written in a revolutionary style mimicking tweets and WhatsApp messages, the novel explores what happens when a wallet gets hacked and 144 ETH disappears, destroying friendships and forcing a confrontation with mortality and meaning beyond "number go up."
